### How Quality Is Measured

Every PRD gets two scores (out of 10):

| Score | Simple Question | What "Good" Looks Like |
|-------|-----------------|------------------------|
| **Translation** | "Did we capture everything from the ticket?" | 8+ means nothing was missed |
| **Value** | "Is this worth building?" | 8+ means strong business case |

**Value considers 5 things**: Customer need (30%), Revenue potential (25%), Strategic alignment (20%), Efficiency gains (15%), Competitive advantage (10%)

**Threshold**: Both scores must be 7+ or the PRD goes back for improvement
