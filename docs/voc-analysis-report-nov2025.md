## Executive Summary

Analysis of 31 customer feedback entries from SGProof.com reveals significant performance and usability issues following a recent app/website update. The primary concerns center around:

1. **Performance/Speed Issues** - Most critical and frequent complaint (45% of feedback)
2. **Mobile App Problems** - Specific to iOS mobile experience (35% of feedback)
3. **Checkout/Order Submission Failures** - Critical blocking issues (26% of feedback)
4. **Search/Navigation Problems** - UX friction causing workflow disruption (23% of feedback)
5. **Session/Authentication Issues** - Users forced to repeatedly login (13% of feedback)

**Critical Insight:** 93% of all feedback carries negative sentiment, with average satisfaction scores of 2.4/10 (NPS) and 2.8/10 (CSAT). This indicates a significant product quality crisis requiring immediate intervention.
