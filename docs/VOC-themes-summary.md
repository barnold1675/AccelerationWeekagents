# VOC (Voice of Customer) Feedback Analysis

## Overview
- **Source:** Medallia/SurveySparrow Customer Surveys
- **Period:** November 2025 (4th - 16th)
- **Survey Types:** Post Purchase, Always On, Abandon Checkout

---

## Key Themes Identified

### 1. Performance & Speed Issues (Most Critical)
**Frequency:** Very High

**Customer Quotes:**
- "Website is slow and not responsive. Your dev team needs to focus on optimizing speed"
- "The site is getting very slow to load, I'd prefer less flash faster loads"
- "Too slow and too many changes"
- "Very very laggy"
- "It's very slow and used to be easy and fast"

**Tags:** Speed, Issues

---

### 2. App Quality & Stability
**Frequency:** High

**Customer Quotes:**
- "Mobile app is garbage"
- "Your new app and online ordering portal are garbage - they time out, don't bring up order numbers"
- "Since you updated my proof app sucks! Can't get products to show and cuts me out before done ordering"
- "I hate the updated app"
- "The app is a little slow and glitches sometimes"

**Tags:** App, Issues

---

### 3. Usability & UX Problems
**Frequency:** High

**Customer Quotes:**
- "Proof website needs a lot of love. Very unfriendly for users to use"
- "Website needs a lot of work - tons of missing pictures, overly complicated ways to search for past purchases and saved items"
- "I found the new update to be less intuitive than the old interface"
- "Please fix when you scroll and select an item - it should bring you back to where you left off"
- "Glitches all over the place and is not user friendly"

**Tags:** Usability (UX), Issues

---

### 4. Search Functionality Issues
**Frequency:** Medium-High

**Customer Quotes:**
- "The search function is not working well on the update"
- "Half the time it doesn't show up (nothing shows up) or the thing you're looking for isn't the first thing shown"
- "I have to sign out of the app every time I look up something different"

**Tags:** Issues, Search

---

### 5. Cart & Checkout Problems
**Frequency:** Medium

**Customer Quotes:**
- "While check out the bottle cost disappears!"
- "Can't submit order"
- "Orders go through most of the time - there have been 2 instances where I put an order in but it didn't fully submit"
- "Will not let me place order"

**Tags:** Cart/Checkout, Issues

---

### 6. Navigation & State Management
**Frequency:** Medium

**Customer Quotes:**
- "After ordering promo item it goes back to main screen instead of staying on that same product list"
- "Seems like you've got to hit a lot of back buttons after accepting a proof proposal before you can get to your cart"

**Tags:** Issues, Usability (UX)

---

### 7. Authentication Issues
**Frequency:** Low-Medium

**Customer Quotes:**
- "Hard to sign on"
- "I have tried several times to reset password and it still won't work"
- "Do not work well with my iPhone 16 pro max - I have to log in and out to search for the next product"

**Tags:** Issues

## Priority Recommendations for PRD

### P0 - Critical (Immediate)
1. **Performance Optimization** - Address slow loading, lag, timeouts
2. **App Stability** - Fix crashes, glitches, session timeouts

### P1 - High Priority
3. **Search Improvements** - Better relevance, reliability
4. **Cart/Checkout Reliability** - Ensure orders submit correctly
5. **Navigation State** - Return users to scroll position after viewing items

### P2 - Medium Priority
6. **UX Simplification** - Reduce complexity, improve intuitiveness
7. **Authentication Flow** - Simplify login, fix password reset
8. **Missing Content** - Address missing product images
